# Positioning: Enterprise vs Mid-Market

## Recommended External Positioning
Lead with mid-market workflows that are fully demoable today. Present enterprise support as implemented architecture under active hardening.

## Mid-Market (Lead)
- Frontend-backed workflow demo available now.
- HITL PO operations and alert handling are operational.
- Reproducible iteration logs and model registry artifacts are present.
- Faster onboarding narrative and clearer pilot path.

## Enterprise (Position as "Validated + Hardening")
- EDI/SFTP/event adapters exist in code.
- Productization contract artifacts and schema lineage snapshots exist.
- Enterprise parser/runtime quality gates are being tightened before claiming full SLA readiness.

## Talk Track (Use Verbatim)
"Today, the live workflow is real and demoable: alerts, forecasts, purchase decisions, and governed model operations. Pilot next is operator visibility, integration resilience, and release confidence for a first SMB rollout. Enterprise adapters and governance patterns are implemented to prove technical depth, but I do not claim they are fully enterprise-ready today."

## What Not To Claim Until Gates Pass
- "All tests pass" across all suites.
- "Enterprise ingestion fully production-hardened."
- "Fully automated production retraining flow proven end-to-end in cloud."
